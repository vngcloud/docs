# vDNS

In the digital era, the speed and stability of websites and online applications are critical factors that determine user experience and business success. One of the key components affecting access speed is the Domain Name System (DNS). Understanding this, GreenNode proudly introduces vDNS — a powerful DNS solution designed to optimize performance, enhance security, and ensure the stability of your online infrastructure.

### What is vDNS?

vDNS is a DNS (Domain Name System) service developed and operated by GreenNode, providing fast and reliable domain name resolution capabilities. With a robust server infrastructure, globally distributed architecture, and advanced technologies, vDNS efficiently converts domain names (for example: example.com) into IP addresses (for example: 192.168.1.1), enabling users to access your websites and applications smoothly and without interruption.

### Outstanding Benefits of vDNS

#### Optimized Performance

vDNS is built on a powerful infrastructure with globally distributed servers, minimizing latency and accelerating domain name resolution speed, delivering a fast access experience for users anywhere in the world.

#### High Availability

With a redundant architecture and automatic failover capabilities, vDNS ensures service continuity and stability while minimizing downtime.

#### Strong Security

vDNS integrates advanced security features such as DDoS protection and DNSSEC, helping protect your DNS system from cybersecurity threats.

#### Easy Management

An intuitive and user-friendly management interface allows you to easily configure and manage your DNS records.

#### Seamless Integration with the GreenNode Ecosystem

vDNS is tightly integrated with other GreenNode services, creating a complete and powerful ecosystem for your online infrastructure.

GreenNode’s experienced technical team is available 24/7 to support you, answer questions, and ensure your services operate smoothly.
